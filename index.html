The State of Unions

Starbucks

Buffalo, NY

In December of 2021 first Starbucks votes to unionize in in Buffalo. They will begin to negotiate a contract for better pay, benefits and working conditions

Amazon

Staten Island, NY

In April of 2022 the first Amazon Warehouse unionizes in Staten Island despite union busting and by the company.

Kellogs

Michigan, Nebraska, Pennsylvania and Tennessee

Kellog's workers strike for 11 hours after a 5 year contract expired. The strike ended in October of 2021 when unionized workers voted on a 5 year contract.

John Deere

Pennsylvania, Illinois, Iowa

John Deere workers go on strike at midnight for a month after their contract expired. They negotiated for higher wages and better benefits as the company has seen a 38% increase in profits over the last 30 years. An agreement was reached for a 10% pay increase.
